## Audio and video media boundary

music2bb does not download, upload, host, or decrypt any audio or video media
content. It does not obtain or save media files, media streams, or encrypted
media payloads, and it provides no media playback, offline media copies,
transcoding, relaying, distribution, hosting, or digital-rights-management
(DRM) circumvention functionality.

The program processes only data required for playlist conversion: publicly
shared playlist pages provided by the user and track metadata such as names,
artists, albums, and durations; Bilibili search-result metadata and identifiers
such as titles, uploader names, and BVIDs; and authenticated operations
explicitly initiated and authorized by the user. “Conversion” means matching
playlist track metadata to metadata for videos that already exist on Bilibili
and adding the selected video identifiers to a user-designated Bilibili
favorites folder. The corresponding third-party services continue to provide
and control all media content.
